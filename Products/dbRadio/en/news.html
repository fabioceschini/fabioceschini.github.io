This app was developed by web radio enthusiasts around the world.

We want everyone to be able to listen to their favorite radio stations at home and at work.

To make this possible, we've invested in a dedicated server to store and share the radio stations registered by the community.

If you like the project, please consider supporting its maintenance and development with a small donation.

Together, we will build the largest community-based web radio database.

dbRadio is and always will be 100% free.
